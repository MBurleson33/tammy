About This Admin Panel

This admin panel lets you update your website content without needing any technical help.

Testimonials — Add, edit, hide, or reorder the quotes on your homepage.
Products — Update prices, descriptions, and show/hide products.
Site Text — Edit the main words on your homepage like the headline and welcome letter.
Orders — See all orders submitted through your website and track their status.
